Goku TimeLine

Age: 720, Goku fought the youthful Demon King Piccolo and managed to beat him with a furious punch.

Age: 742, Goku fought Frieza on Planet Namek and evolved into the legendary Super Saiyan.

Age: 740, The Saiyans Vegeta and Nappa fought the Z Fighters. While 3 of them died, Goku managed to defeat Nappa and Vegeta.

Age: 753, Goku and Vegeta both fought against Majin Buu on the Supreme Kai's Sacred Planet. Using the help of the Earthlings, they managed to eradicate Buu with a Spirit Bomb.
